Welcome to 21 Flyers flying club!

We are one of the oldest aviation clubs in the country, having been in continuous operation since its inception in 19XX.

Our membership of 12 active members is composed of a diverse group of pilots spanning the breadth of aviation experience.

We pride ourselves on setting high standards for flight safety, pilot proficiency and proper care and maintenance of our equipment.

Members of the club are equity owners in a 1976 Cessna 172.

Social activities include monthly meetings and cross country flights that encourage member participation and fellowship.
