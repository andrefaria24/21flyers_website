Please use freq. 123.05 to self-announce on the Hudson River. Turn on every light you have and maintain the right side of the river below 1000 ASL. Be especially careful to watch for helicopter traffic between JRA (near the Empire State Building) and the Statue of Liberty. If you fly between the Lady and Newark airport, you must be at or below 500 ASL, and at least 500 feet away from "any person, vessel, or object."
