Joining the club






 

You do not need to have a pilot's license to join our club.

Members have joined the club as student pilots while taking lessons from one of the club's instructors.

Club dues cover the non-flying expenses of the club (insurance, hangar fees, et cetera). The hourly aircraft usage fees include the cost of gas, oil, etc. Pilots pay only for time that the engine is running.

For new members, the cost to join includes a non-refundable initiation fee as well as an membership investment equity.

Fees are listed in the Membership Application form which can be downloaded and completed.

Club members are covered by club's aircraft liability and damage insurance. Aviation renter's insurance is not needed since you are a member of the Club.
 

Candidates must attend one membership meeting and complete the Membership Application.
